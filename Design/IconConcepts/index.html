WorkoutApp Icon Concepts

Four directions built from the product itself: active workout, progress tracking, program structure, and a more brand-led monogram option.

01. Orbit Progress

Timer ring plus progress bars. Closest to the current app UX and statistics/workout mix.

Timer + Progress

02. Rack Stack

Vertical rack/barbell logic with layered plates. Feels most like programs, sets, and gym hardware.

Programs + Sets

03. Motion Plates

Ascending motion path with weight-plate nodes. Strongest sense of movement and progression.

Movement + Growth

04. Monogram Wave

Brand-first W mark with neon energy. Best candidate if you want the icon to feel less literal.
